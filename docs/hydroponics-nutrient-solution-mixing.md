# Hydroponics Nutrient Solution Mixing Instructions

## Container Size

* Use two 1 gallon (3785 mL) food-grade containers
  - I wash and reuse Harris 30% Vinegar Multi-Purpose Cleaner containers purchased from Home Depot
* Fill each container approximately half full with hot (not boiling) water

---

# 🧪 Part A — MasterBlend + Epsom Salt

## Ingredients

* MasterBlend 4-18-38: **910 grams**
* Epsom Salt (Magnesium Sulfate): **455 grams**

## Instructions

1. Add both nutrients to the container labeled **Part A**
2. Shake or stir until dissolved
3. Fill with hot water to the 1 gallon mark
4. Continue mixing until all nutrients are fully dissolved
5. Verify no solids remain on the bottom of the container

---

# 🧪 Part B — Calcium Nitrate

## Ingredients

* Calcium Nitrate: **910 grams**

## Instructions

1. Add Calcium Nitrate to the container labeled **Part B**
2. Shake or stir until dissolved
3. Fill with hot water to the 1 gallon mark
4. Continue mixing until fully dissolved
5. Verify no solids remain on the bottom of the container

---

# ⚠️ Mixing Precautions

* **Do NOT mix Part A and Part B concentrates together directly**
* Always dose Part A and Part B separately into the hydroponic system
* Shake or stir concentrate containers before each use
* Store containers sealed in a cool location out of direct sunlight

---

# 📏 Dosing Ratio

* Dose **10 mL of Part A** per gallon of fresh water added
* Dose **10 mL of Part B** per gallon of fresh water added

## Target EC

* Recommended EC range: **1.8 to 2.0 mS/cm**

---

# 🧼 Maintenance Notes

* If algae begins growing inside the containers, excessive light exposure is likely occurring
* Discard contaminated solution and remix a fresh batch
* Replace nutrient solution if:

  * solution becomes cloudy
  * excessive settling occurs
  * contamination is suspected
